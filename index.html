Our Platform

Vaultavo is a biometric smartcard-based hardware private cryptographic key storage device and Hardware Security Module (HSM) ecosystem and platform. The Vaultavo card provides biometric authentication and is credit-card profile that manages all of a clients blockchain private keys without the need for recovery seeds. Together with a portfolio of unique, value-added features and services, the Vaultavo card works to simplify the process of securely storing and transacting with all digital assets. The Vaultavo card can be used standalone, or as part of the Vaultavo custody platform with the Vaultavo card HSM ecosystem. Unlike other providers, Vaultavo uses unique and proprietary card Hardware Security Modules (HSM), making our custody solution entirely closed-loop and offering greater security - without the risks of providers who rely on third-party technology solutions.

Our Smartcard

The Vaultavo uniquely designed and manufactured smartcard is a bank grade security smartcard which completely protects each client’s Blockchain Private Keys and fits in a wallet/purse

  • All Private Keys are securely created, stored, managed and used within the card and are never exposed in any way to anyone – no recovery words or sentences are required to recreate your private key
  • Biometric fingerprint authentication and authorization of the cardholder and their chosen beneficiaries
  • eInk display and LEDs for cardholder visual verification and guidance
  • Multiple interfaces – contact, contactless and USB
  • On-card value-added features like Emergency Mode Service, After Life Service and Lost / Stolen Card Service

Our Card Hardware Security Module (HSM)

The Vaultavo proprietary designed and manufactured card HSM is a 2U rackmount case that holds hundreds of the Vaultavo Smartcards simultaneously.

A Vaultavo Smartcard is inserted within the card HSM and authority is given to the relevant manager to perform transactions within the smartcard on the client’s behalf - the client’s digital private key is therefore never exposed to anyone.

Once the manager/client relationship ends, the client’s Vaultavo Smartcard is simply removed from the card HSM and returned to the client. The client is then satisfied that his/her digital private keys were never copied or backed-up and that the manager has no further access to his/her crypto assets. The manager also does not have the responsibility of securing a client’s digital private keys, nor the burden of the required processes and proof of destruction - hence no further liability.

This next generation blockchain asset custody service solution is a game changer in the market. Simply sign up for a competitive monthly fee and receive the Vaultavo Smartcards through courier. Perform the secure biometric enrolment procedure on the cards with your clients and insert these cards into our card HSM. Using similar Vaultavo Smartcards; define and enroll your security and financial officers, apply your relevant transaction rules to your clients’ cards and authorization rules to your officers’ cards and your company has a bank grade professional blockchain asset custody solution. It is that simple.
